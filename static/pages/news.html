Fri Jul 20, 2018 : TzScan update
TzScan Update
OCamlPro is pleased to announce the latest update of TZScan (http://tzscan.io), its Tezos block explorer to ease the use of the Tezos network. TzScan is now ready for the protocol update scheduled for tomorrow. In addition to some minor bugfixes, the main novelties are:
  • Displaying of obtained and expected rewards
  • Adding of internal transactions of smart contracts
  • Adding of delegation services
  • Aliasing of known accounts and sponsors
  • Improvements of UX, and faster navigation
  • Improvements on desktop, tablets and mobiles
We continue to maintain the alphanet and zeronet branches in parallel of the betanet.
We keep on working hard to improve and add new features to TzScan. Further enhancements and optimizations are to come. Enjoy and play with our explorer!
If you have any suggestions or bugs to report, please notify us at contact@tzscan.io
Wed Jul 11, 2018 : TzScan update
TzScan Update
We have fixed several problems with baking rights : baking slots should be correctly ordered, deposit/bonds should take into account the ramping-up during the first cycles. We also added many new features, enjoy !
Mon Jul 9, 2018 : TzScan update
TzScan Update
We have added many new features since the Launch: the possibility to see the staking balance early in delegates' accounts, estimation of cycle end, pages with resources, etc. Feedback is always welcome !
Sat Jun 30, 2018 : TzScan is ready for the Launch
Tezos Launch
TzScan has switched to the betanet branch of Tezos. It is probably the best place to watch the Launch of Tezos, from everywhere in the world !

Incidents

Mon Jul 9, 2018 10:00 CEST: crawler downtime for 15 minutes
Crawler Down
Our Tezos crawler failed this morning, at the end of cycle 2. This was caused by some specific code that is run at the end of each cycle to recover the rolls and baking rights of all users. Once the problem was identified, the node and the crawler were restarted and the system was up-to-date in seconds.
