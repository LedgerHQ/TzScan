TzScan, the Block Explorer for Tezos.

TzScan is a block explorer for Tezos, written by the OCamlPro team.

Compared to other block explorers, TzScan does not only display the raw content of the blockchain, but tries to interpret this content and show its meaning for the network. Proof-of-stake and delegations become easier to grasp this way. The prototype of Tezos was initially designed and developed at OCamlPro since 2014.
